Mint Lemonade Recipe

Watch Video

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 Lemonade
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup Water
  • A handful of fresh mint leaves
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the sugar and 1 cup of water. Heat over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar has dissolved. Remove from heat and let cool.
  2. In a blender or food processor, combine the lemon juice, sugar syrup, mint leaves, and 4 cups of cold water. Blend until the mint is finely chopped.
  3. Pour the lemonade into a pitcher and add ice. Serve chilled, garnished with additional mint leaves if desired.
    Enjoy!
